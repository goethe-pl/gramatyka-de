Add-on for Microsoft Word

We offer an add-on for Microsoft Word 2016 or later. Works on Windows, Mac, and Word Online.

  • Install from AppSource

  • Step 1

    With the help of the Microsoft Word add-in you can use langpress.com directly in Word. After installing the add-in, you will see the “LT” icon on the “Home” tab.

  • Step 2

    Use your langpress.com account to log in.

  • Step 3

    Select whether you want to check a paragraph or the whole document.

  • Step 4

    In the list of possible errors, click a correction and it will be applied to your text.
